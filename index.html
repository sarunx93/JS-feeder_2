About US

Feeder was formed back in 2014 with a purpose of participating shows in Assumtion University (Thailand). The band is known for its loud but exciting performance which aims to move the crowd, both their bodies and minds. The biggest achievement was to win AU Music Contest in 2016, even though none of the members were awarded with best musician trophies.

The band is currently inactive due to the members are on their own journeys hence unlikely to reunite. It does not mean we can't expect them, though.
